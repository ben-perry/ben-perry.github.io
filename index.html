Realtime GI Prototype in Unity

Before working on my game Adrift Arena, I prototyped an idea I had on how to achieve realtime global illumination. It combines the ideas of Forward+ and irradiance probes. The pixel shader gets GI information from a camera view frustum grid. Each cell in this 3D frustum grid is assigned what irradiance probes it overlaps with.
